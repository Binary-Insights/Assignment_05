## Company Overview

Anysphere, Inc., operating under the brand name Cursor, is a private AI startup headquartered in San Francisco, California, United States. The company was founded in 2021 and focuses on developing AI-driven tools for software development. Its website is www.anysphere.co.

## Business Model and GTM

Anysphere's business model revolves around providing AI-powered software development tools. Their product lineup includes Cursor, Cursor Enterprise, Cursor Agents, Composer, and Cloud Agents. These products are designed to enhance software engineering by delegating coding tasks to AI, allowing developers to focus on strategic direction. The company states that its products are purpose-built for working with agents, emphasizing speed and intelligence in software engineering.
