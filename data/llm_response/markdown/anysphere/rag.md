## Company Overview

Anysphere is a private company headquartered in San Francisco, California. It operates within the software development industry, focusing on artificial intelligence-powered coding platforms designed to enhance the productivity and capabilities of programmers and software engineers. The company is known for its product, Cursor, which aims to automate coding and improve the efficiency of software development. As of 2025, Anysphere employs approximately 300 people.

## Business Model and GTM

Anysphere's business model revolves around developing AI tools and assistants to aid human programmers. The company states its mission is to build a "human-AI programmer" that is significantly more effective than a single programmer. This hybrid engineer is designed to have effortless control over their codebase, enabling rapid iteration even in complex systems. Anysphere's product, Cursor, is used by tens of thousands of enterprises, including notable companies like Salesforce, NVIDIA, and PwC, to accelerate product development and enhance software durability.

## Funding & Investor Profile

Anysphere has raised a total of $2.3 billion in its Series D funding round, achieving a valuation of $29.3 billion. The round was co-led by Accel. Prior to this, the company raised $900 million in a Series C round. Other investors include Thrive Capital and two additional undisclosed investors. The company has completed five funding rounds in total.
