## Company Overview

ElevenLabs is a global leader in AI voice technology, with a focus on providing innovative audio solutions. The company has expanded its operations internationally, establishing a subsidiary in Japan and a European headquarters in London. ElevenLabs aims to be the first choice for AI audio solutions, leveraging a team experienced in startups and high-growth environments.

## Business Model and GTM

ElevenLabs offers a range of AI-driven products and solutions, including Text to Speech (TTS), Speech to Text, Voice Changer, and AI Music Generator. The company targets various sectors such as retail, e-commerce, telecommunications, financial services, and technology. Their go-to-market strategy includes partnerships with enterprises and startups, as well as offering resources like APIs and product guides to developers and commercial partners.
