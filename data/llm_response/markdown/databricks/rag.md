## Company Overview

Databricks is a data and AI company headquartered in San Francisco, with a mission to simplify and democratize data and AI. The company aims to help data and AI teams solve complex problems by providing a unified platform for data, analytics, and AI. Databricks has a global presence with offices worldwide and collaborates with over 1,200 cloud, ISV, and consulting partners.

## Business Model and GTM

Databricks operates a platform-based business model, offering a unified platform for data management, analytics, and AI. The platform includes features for data warehousing, data engineering, data science, and application development. The company leverages partnerships with cloud providers, technology partners, and consulting integrators to expand its reach and enhance its offerings. Databricks also provides industry-specific solutions across sectors such as financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, and more.
