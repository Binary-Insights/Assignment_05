## Company Overview

Anthropic is an AI research company headquartered in San Francisco, California. It focuses on building reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. The company's flagship product is Claude, an AI assistant designed for tasks at any scale. Anthropic's research spans multiple areas, including natural language processing, human feedback, scaling laws, reinforcement learning, code generation, and interpretability. The company operates as a public benefit corporation, emphasizing AI safety and alignment with human values.
