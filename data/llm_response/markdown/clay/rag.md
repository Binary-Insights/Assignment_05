## Company Overview

Clay is a software development company focused on creating tools for growth teams. The company positions itself as a creative tool for growth, enabling businesses to go to market with unique data and the ability to act on it. Clay's product offerings include features like Claygent for AI-driven research, Sculptor for building GTM workflows, and Signals for tracking job changes and social mentions. The company has a workforce size of 51-200 employees and is active on social media platforms such as LinkedIn and YouTube.

## Business Model and GTM

Clay's business model revolves around providing AI-powered tools that automate sales prospecting and research, growth marketing, and CRM data enrichment. The company offers products like Sculptor and Sequencer to build GTM workflows and automate personalized email outreach. Clay serves a diverse customer base, including notable companies like OpenAI, Vanta, Verkada, Sendoso, Anthropic, and Rippling. The company emphasizes its ability to help businesses scale their GTM motions and growth experiments.

## Funding & Investor Profile

Clay completed a Series C expansion fundraise at a $3.1 billion valuation in 2025. The company is backed by prominent investors, including Elena Verna (Head of Growth, Dropbox), Scott Belsky (CPO, Adobe), and Stevie Case (CRO, Vanta). Sequoia led a $1.5 billion tender offer for Clay, highlighting strong investor confidence in the company's potential.

## Growth Momentum
