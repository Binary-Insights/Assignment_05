## Company Overview

Glean AI is a company focused on empowering finance teams to manage and optimize their spending. The company provides tools for spend management, vendor management, and financial analytics. Glean AI offers features such as automated line item extraction, variance analysis, spend forecasts, and vendor pricing benchmarking. The company is led by CEO Howard Katzenberg and Head of Data Ankur Patel.

## Business Model and GTM

Glean AI operates a SaaS business model, providing spend management solutions to finance teams. The company offers integrations with popular accounting software such as Quickbooks, Xero, Sage Intacct, NetSuite, and Dynamics. Glean AI's go-to-market strategy includes offering demos and leveraging partnerships to reach potential customers. The company emphasizes the ability to save money and increase accountability for finance teams.

## Growth Momentum

The company states that its solutions have led to significant time savings and cost reductions for its clients. For example, Elisa Rodriguez, Sr. Manager of FP&A at Orum, reports saving 50% of an FTE in monthly time savings and identifying billing issues that would have otherwise gone unnoticed.
