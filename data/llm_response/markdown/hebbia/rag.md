## Company Overview

Hebbia is an AI startup focused on developing software products for the finance sector. The company is committed to building what it describes as "the most important software product of the next 100 years." Hebbia's team comprises top talent with experience from premier startups, financial services, and legal firms. The company is based in New York City and offers a range of employee benefits, including start-up equity, competitive salaries, and comprehensive insurance.

## Business Model and GTM

Hebbia's business model revolves around providing AI solutions tailored for finance, law, and corporate sectors. The company offers products that enhance workflows in investment banking, private equity, credit, and public equities. Hebbia's go-to-market strategy includes direct engagement with financial institutions, as evidenced by partnerships with firms managing a total of $14 trillion in assets under management (AUM). The company also emphasizes security and privacy, ensuring that user data is not used for training its AI models.
