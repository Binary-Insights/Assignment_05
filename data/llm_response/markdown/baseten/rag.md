## Company Overview

Baseten is a company focused on building products for engineering and machine learning teams. The company offers a platform that includes dedicated deployments, model APIs, training, inference stack, model runtimes, and infrastructure solutions. Baseten provides multi-cloud capacity management and a developer experience that includes model management and deployment options such as Baseten Cloud, self-hosted, and hybrid solutions. The company also offers solutions for enterprise needs, including transcription, image generation, text-to-speech, large language models, compound AI, and embeddings.

## Business Model and GTM

The company states that it builds "delightful products" for engineering and machine learning teams to deliver their best work. Baseten's platform and solutions are designed to cater to developers and enterprises, providing a range of deployment options and model management capabilities. The company offers resources such as documentation, a model library, and a changelog to support developers.
