## Company Overview

Baseten is an AI infrastructure platform based in San Francisco, led by CEO Tuhin Srivastava. The company specializes in providing the tools, expertise, and hardware necessary to bring AI products to market efficiently. Baseten's proprietary Inference Stack offers high performance and reliability, ensuring global availability with 99.99% uptime. The company operates in the software development industry and employs between 51-200 people.

## Business Model and GTM

Baseten focuses on delivering infrastructure solutions for machine learning model deployment. The company provides a platform that allows data scientists to deploy ML models with minimal code, offering scalable APIs without the typical infrastructure challenges. Baseten's offerings include dedicated deployments, model APIs, and training services, catering to engineering and machine learning teams aiming to optimize their workflows and scale AI applications.

## Funding & Investor Profile

Baseten has completed five funding rounds, with the most recent being a Series D round in March 2025, where it raised $150 million at a $2.15 billion valuation. This round was led by BOND, with participation from CapitalG, Premji, Kevin and Elizabeth Weil, and existing investors such as IVP, Spark Capital, and Greylock. The company has raised over $285 million in total funding.

## Growth Momentum
