## Company Overview

Coactive AI is a private company founded in 2021, specializing in building multimodal AI systems that facilitate collaboration between humans and machines. The company is composed of a diverse team of over 30 experts with extensive experience in machine learning systems and data-centric AI. Coactive AI operates from three global hub offices.

## Business Model and GTM

Coactive AI focuses on providing AI solutions that enhance content workflows, particularly in the media and entertainment industry. Their platform offers capabilities such as visual AI search, metadata tagging, and analytics. The company emphasizes a future-proof approach with customizable models that are upgrade-ready, aiming to accelerate content workflows and unlock the potential of visual content. The company states that their solutions are designed to maximize content in the AI era.
