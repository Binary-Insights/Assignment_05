## Company Overview

Coactive Systems Inc., branded as Coactive AI, was founded in 2021. The company is a private AI startup that focuses on machine learning systems and AI. Coactive AI operates with a team of over 30 members and has established three global hub offices. The company specializes in developing AI platforms for video understanding and content discovery.

## Business Model and GTM

Coactive AI offers two main products: a Multimodal AI platform for video understanding and a Content Discovery tool. The Multimodal AI platform provides context-aware analysis across video segments, going beyond simple shot detection. It includes features like built-in lineage tracing for asset and transformation tracking. The Content Discovery tool enables robust metadata generation from visual and audio content, using natural language prompts for flexible tagging. The company states that these products are built for scale, suggesting a focus on enterprise-level clients.
