## Company Overview

World Labs is a frontier AI research and product company specializing in spatial intelligence. The company focuses on building foundational world models that can perceive, generate, reason, and interact with the 3D world. Founded by visionary AI pioneer Fei-Fei Li, along with Justin Johnson, Christoph Lassner, and Ben Mildenhall, World Labs aims to revolutionize artificial intelligence by developing Large World Models that extend AI capabilities beyond language and 2D visuals into complex 3D environments.

## Business Model and GTM

The company states that its first product, Marble, is powered by generative 3D world models, enabling users to create spatially cohesive, high-fidelity, and persistent 3D worlds from a single image, video, or text prompt. World Labs positions itself as a leader in spatial intelligence, unlocking new forms of storytelling, creativity, design, simulation, and immersive experiences across both virtual and physical worlds.
