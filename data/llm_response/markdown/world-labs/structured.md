## Company Overview

WORLD LABS TECHNOLOGIES, headquartered in San Francisco, CA, operates in the Artificial Intelligence sector. The company is focused on developing Large World Models (LWMs) to revolutionize artificial intelligence by enabling systems to perceive, generate, and interact with the 3D world.

## Business Model and GTM

The company states that its mission is to revolutionize artificial intelligence through the development of Large World Models. These models are designed to enhance spatial intelligence by generating 3D worlds from a single image and enabling real-time video generation as users interact with the system. The specific go-to-market strategy is not disclosed.

## Funding & Investor Profile

WORLD LABS TECHNOLOGIES has raised more than $230 million in total funding. The Series C expansion was completed at a $3.1 billion valuation, with significant investment from Andreessen Horowitz, led by Martin Casado and Sarah Wang.

## Growth Momentum

The company has released several innovative products, including a research preview of RTFM, a generative world model for real-time video interaction, and an AI system for generating 3D worlds from a single image.
