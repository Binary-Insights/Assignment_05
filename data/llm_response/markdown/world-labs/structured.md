## Company Overview

World Labs, legally known as World Labs, is a private company headquartered in San Francisco, California, United States. The company operates in the fields of spatial intelligence, AI research, and product development. World Labs positions itself as a leading spatial intelligence company, focusing on building frontier world models capable of perceiving, generating, reasoning, and interacting with the 3D world. The company is also involved in AI research and product development.

## Business Model and GTM

World Labs is a product company that develops advanced AI models and tools. Its flagship product, Marble, is a generative 3D world model that allows users to create spatially cohesive, high-fidelity, and persistent 3D worlds from a single image, video, or text prompt. The company states that its products enable creators and developers to explore the next frontier of AI through spatial, world-model-driven experiences.
