## Company Overview

World Labs is a private company specializing in spatial intelligence, AI research, and product development. The company is focused on revolutionizing artificial intelligence by developing Large World Models, which extend AI capabilities beyond language and 2D visuals into complex 3D environments, both virtual and real. World Labs is recognized as a leading spatial intelligence company, building frontier world models that can perceive, generate, reason, and interact with the 3D world.

## Business Model and GTM

The company states that its flagship product, Marble, is powered by best-in-class generative 3D world models. Marble enables users to create spatially cohesive, high-fidelity, and persistent 3D worlds from a single image, video, or text prompt. The company has also released a research preview of RTFM, a generative world model that generates video in real-time as users interact with it. World Labs is exploring the next frontier of AI through spatial, world-model-driven experiences, which are designed to empower creators and developers.
