## Company Overview

WORLD LABS TECHNOLOGIES, operating under the brand name World Labs, is headquartered in San Francisco, CA. The company is positioned in the fields of spatial intelligence, AI research, and product development. World Labs is recognized as a leading spatial intelligence company, focusing on building advanced world models that can perceive, generate, reason, and interact with the 3D world.

## Business Model and GTM

World Labs is engaged in the development of AI systems that enable the creation of 3D worlds from minimal inputs such as a single image, video, or text prompt. Their flagship product, Marble, exemplifies this capability by allowing users to generate spatially cohesive, high-fidelity, and persistent 3D environments. The company states that its technology is at the frontier of spatial intelligence, offering new possibilities for creators and developers.

## Funding & Investor Profile

World Labs has secured over $230 million in total funding. The funding round was led by prominent investors including Martin Casado and Sarah Wang from Andreessen Horowitz, Scott Sandell from NEA, and Jordan Jacobs from Radical Ventures. This strong backing from leading venture capital firms underscores the confidence in World Labs' innovative approach and market potential.

## Growth Momentum

World Labs has demonstrated significant progress in its product development, with multiple product releases showcasing advancements in 3D world generation. Notable releases include an AI system capable of generating 3D worlds from a single image and the introduction of Marble, a multimodal world model available to the public. These developments highlight the company's commitment to pushing the boundaries of spatial intelligence.
